Instructions
The value to be entered in the input boxes should be based on the follow:
  • Value starts at top left corner and continues clockwise
  • First comes a letter indicating the direction of the vehicle: left, right or straight (l,r,d)
  • Then a dot (".") after which follows the name, usually a number (ex. r.1)
  • Then follows a comma (",") indicating we're switching to the next vehicle.
    Vehicles are left to right and at most two vehicles per corner
  • Then follows a semicolon (";") indicating we're switching to the next corner.
    At most four corners
  • Vehicles, names and even corners can be omitted
Complete Maquette Code example:
l.1,r.2;l.3,d.4;d.5,d.6;r.7
